Putin appears to have done better than the 58-59 pct exit polling:

RESULTS

As of 05:30 Moscow time, and with 95 percent of the vote counted, the CEC announced the following national figures:
Putin — 64.2 percent, Zyuganov — 17/1 percent, Prokhorov — 7.4 percent, Zhirinovskiy — 6.2 percent, and Mironov — 3.8
percent.

Among interesting regional figures:
Min Moscow, as of 04:00 and with almost 13 percent of the vote counted, Putin had 49.9 percent, Zyuganov and
Prokhorov both with about 18.4 percent, Zhirinovskiy — 6.5 and Mironov — 4.8 percent. Turnout was 61.7 percent.

Ellin the Russian Far East, with almost all the votes counted, Putin had 57.4 percent, Zyuganov — 20.4, Zhirinovskiy — 8.6,
Prokhorov — 7.8 and Mironov — 4.3.

IllAs expected, Putin dominated the North Caucasus, with initial results showing him receiving 99.8 percent in Chechnya,
92.8 in Dagestan, and 91.5 in Ingushetiya.
