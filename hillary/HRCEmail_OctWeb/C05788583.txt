MONTERREY (AP) - Officials in northern Mexico say a prison riot has left about 20 people dead.
   Nuevo Leon state public security spokesman Jorge Domene Zambrano tells a local television station that the
riot broke out at about 2 a.m. at a prison in the city of Apodaca. He said authorities at the state prosecutor's
office reported 20 dead.
