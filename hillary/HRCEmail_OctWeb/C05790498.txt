           2012 Class of Fellows and Foreign Honorary Members Elected
          The Academy announced today the election of leaders in the sciences, the humanities and the arts,
          business, public affairs, and the nonprofit sector. The new class of 201 Fellows and 17 Foreign Honorary
          Members comes from 23 states and 10 countries and represents nearly 100 institutions.

          The new members will be inducted at a ceremony on October 6, 2012, in Cambridge, Massachusetts.
