Subject: Four Explosions Around Protest Site in Bangkok




(SBU) The explosions occurred around 2100 local time, according to press. The cause and number of casualties are
unknown. Post personnel reported unidentified assailants fired what were likely M79 grenades into the Sala Daeng sky
train (BTS) station. There are no reports of Amcit injuries at this time. The Sala Daeng train station, which is located about
500 meters from the end of the red-shirt encampment and is the location of recent confrontations over the past several
evenings, has been temporarily closed. Post will convene an EAC on the morning of April 23, to further review the
situation.
