DUBAI (Reuters) - Bahrain's interior ministry said on Wednesday it had destroyed a package containing
explosives sent to the country's airport from Britain via Dubai.

  The ministry said it was investigating the incident. Bahrain said on Sunday an explosion outside the British
embassy was due to a bomb placed under a vehicle nearby.
