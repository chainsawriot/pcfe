Directions to the USNA Chapel through Gate 8

Everyone over the age of 16 must have a valid photo ID

Please enter the Naval Academy only through Gate 8

Inform the Sentry at the Gate you are attending the memorial service for RADM David Stone.

From Washington, DC, take Route 50 East towards the Bay Bridge.

Take Exit #24 to Route 70 Rowe Blvd/Bestgate Road/Annapolis. Bear right. This will put you on Rowe Blvd.

Turn left at second light onto Taylor Avenue.

Go through blinking light at the firehouse. Road comes to a stop sign at a T junction. Turn right and then get in left lane.

Go straight ahead through the light at King George Street. The next light is Gate 8. Turn right into the Naval
Academy. Inform the Sentry at the Gate you are attending the service of RADM David Stone.

Proceed down Bowyer Road, which becomes Decatur Road on the far side of the bridge across College Creek. Continue

Handicap parking will be in front of the Administration Building to the right of the Chapel when looking at the Chapel
entrance. Wheelchairs must enter through the John Paul Jones Crypt. Proceed to the front steps of the Admin Building,

you will find the elevator....if you go through the inner glass doors into the crypt you went too far for the elevator to the
Main Chapel.
