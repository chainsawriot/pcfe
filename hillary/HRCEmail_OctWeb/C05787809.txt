Background:
       The annual UN Population Award provides an important opportunity to raise awareness
of global population issues. Each year two awards are presented — one to an individual and one
to an institution — in recognition of outstanding contributions to the advancement of population-
related issues and to improving the health and welfare of individuals, with a focus on women
and girls.
