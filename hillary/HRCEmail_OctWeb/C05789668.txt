Subject: SKorea says Pyongyang has fired long-range rocket (AP)

 SEOUL, South Korea - (AP) South Korea's Defense Ministry says North Korea has fired a long-range
rocket.
  Defense Ministry spokesman Kim Min-seok told reporters in a nationally televised news conference that the
rocket was fired at 7:39 a.m.
  There was no word from Pyongyang about the launch.
