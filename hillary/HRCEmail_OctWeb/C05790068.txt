Subject: Hardline Burma vice president resigns, says report (Reuters)



RANGOON (Reuters) - A former top general close to Burma's retired dictator Than Shwe resigned as vice-
president, Burmese media reported, ending the hardliner's role in the reformist government.

   Tin Aung Myint Oo, 61, submitted his resignation May 3 for health reasons after returning from Singapore
for medical treatment, the Burma language service of Voice of America reported May 6.

      The report could not be immediately confirmed.
