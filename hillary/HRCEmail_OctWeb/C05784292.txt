Subject: Afghan interior minister survives assassination bid (Reuters)

Oct 23 (Reuters) - The Afghan interior minister survived an assassination attempt in Parwan province north of
Kabul on Sunday, a spokesman for the provincial governor said.

   The minister's bodyguards shot dead a would-be suicide bomber who approached him while he had broken a
journey in order to pray, the spokesman said.
