TUNIS (Reuters) - An Arab force should be created to impose peace in Syria and allow in aid, Qatari Foreign
Minister Sheikh Hamad bin Jassim al-Thani said.

"There is a need to create an Arab force and open humanitarian corridors to provide security to the Syrian
people," he said in a speech at the "Friends of Syria" international meeting in Tunisia.
