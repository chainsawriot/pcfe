Subject: ElBaradei will not contest Egypt presidency-source (Reuters)


CAIRO, Jan 14 (Reuters) - Mohamed ElBaradei will not run in Egypt's presidential election scheduled for later
this year and will announce his withdrawal later on Saturday, a source on his campaign team told Reuters.

   "ElBaradei will announce his withdrawal from the presidency bid," the source, speaking on condition of
anonymity, said.

   The announcement would be made at a news conference called by ElBaradei, a former head of the U.N.
nuclear watchdog.
