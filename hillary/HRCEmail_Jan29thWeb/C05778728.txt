(SBU) Embassy Manama reports a convoy of Saudi armor arrived in Manama at approximately 0900 EDT and is the third
such convoy in the last 24 hours. The convoy included 25 tanks, 40 humvees, 17 tracked armored personnel carriers, 45
trucks carrying gear, 6 fuel trucks, and a bulldozer.
