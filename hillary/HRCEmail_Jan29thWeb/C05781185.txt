(SBU) The Office of the Special Envoy for Sudan reports Pa'gan Amum signed for the Sudan People's
Liberation Movement, Idris Abdelgadir signed for the National Congress Party, and African Union Panel on
Sudan Chair Mbeki signed as a witness to the agreement on new interim security and administrative
arrangements for Abyei. The deal also includes agreement on the withdrawal of Sudanese Armed Forces from
Abyei and the deployment of Ethiopian peacekeepers there.
