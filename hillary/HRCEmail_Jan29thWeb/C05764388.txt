Subject: Nicaraguan Supreme Court rules Ortega can run for re-election




(SBU) Embassy Managua reports the Nicaraguan Supreme Court, all Sandinista judges, ruled that Article 147 of the
Nicaraguan constitution prohibiting sitting presidents from running for re-election was unconstitutional. This allows current
president Daniel Ortega to run for re-election. The opposition has called for demonstrations and protests, however post
does not anticipate violence.
