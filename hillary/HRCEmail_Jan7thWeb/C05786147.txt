                               Statement of Facts

 December 1, 2011

 To Whom It May Concern:


 Mr. Shane Bauer




 Our records indicate that Mr. Bauer was detained by Iranian authorities on July 31, 2009 after
 he strayed across an unmarked border separating Iran from Iraq's northern Kurdistan region.
 After being detained in Evin prison in Tehran for more than two years, Mr. Bauer was charged
 on August 21, 2011 with espidnage and illegal entry and given an eight year sentence. On
 September 21, 2011, one month after his sentence, Mr. Bauer was released and allowed to
 return to the United States.
