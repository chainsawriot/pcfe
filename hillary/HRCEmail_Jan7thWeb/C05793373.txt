                   Readout of the President's call with Egyptian Field Marshal Tantawi

President Obama called Egyptian Field Marshal Tantawi today to reaffirm the close partnership
between the United States and Egypt and to underscore the United States' support for Egypt's
transition to democracy. He welcomed the historic seating of the lower house of Egypt's Parliament
and offered his congratulations to the Egyptian people on taking this important step towards

Egypt and the International Monetary Fund on an economic program that can garner the broad

continuing to cooperate closely on counter-terrorism and regional security issues. They agreed to
remain in close touch in the months ahead as Egypt continues its transition to democracy.
