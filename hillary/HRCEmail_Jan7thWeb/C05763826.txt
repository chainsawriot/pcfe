                                                Honduras Task Force TFH01
                                                           Wednesday, July 22, 2009

DEMONSTRATIONS BLOCK ROADS IN TEGUCIGALPA
(SBU) Embassy Tegucigalpa notes approximately 800 Zelaya supporters gathered on a main road and blocked
traffic in the capital. Separately, approximately 5,000 Micheletti demonstrators marched to the national
stadium, blocking roads along the way.
