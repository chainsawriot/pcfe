Glenn H. Hutchins and Terry J. Lundgren join New York Fed Board of Directors

The New York Fed announced that Glenn H. Hutchins, co-founder and co-chief executive of Silver Lake, has
been appointed a Class B director of the New York Fed starting August 31 for the unexpired portion of a three-
year term ending December 2012. He will serve the remainder of Jeffrey B. Kindler's term on the board of
directors. Terry J. Lundgren, chairman, president and chief executive officer of Macy' s, Inc. has also been
appointed a Class B director starting August 31 for the unexpired portion of a three-year term ending December
11, 2011. He will serve the remainder of Jeffrey R. Immelt's term.
