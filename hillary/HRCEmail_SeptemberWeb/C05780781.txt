Subject: Correction: Saudi Prince Khalid death

   RIYADH (AP) - In a June 11 story, The Associated Press erroneously reported that Saudi Prince Khalid bin
Abdullah bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, King Abdullah's eldest son, had died. The man who died was Khalid bin
Abdullah bin Saud bin Abdulaziz, the grand-nephew of the king.
