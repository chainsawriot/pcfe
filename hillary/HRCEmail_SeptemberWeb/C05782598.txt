Subject: Israel says seeks US help in guarding Cairo embassy (Reuters)

JERUSALEM, Sept 10 (Reuters) - Israel appealed for American help in guarding its Cairo embassy after
Egyptian protestors stormed the building housing it on Friday.

  A statement from Israeli Defence Minister Ehud Barak said that in conversations with U.S. counterpart Leon
Panetta and Obama administration envoy Dennis Ross he had "asked them to protect the embassy from the
demonstrators".
