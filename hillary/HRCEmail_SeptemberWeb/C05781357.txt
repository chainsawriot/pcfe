Subject: Britain to expel all Libyan embassy staff (Reuters)



LONDON (Reuters) - Britain is to expel all Libyan embassy staff from the country, a Foreign Office spokesman
said on Wednesday.

   "We can confirm that the Libyan charge d'affaires has been called to the Foreign and Commonwealth Office
and is being informed that he and the remaining Libyan diplomats in the UK are being expelled," the
spokesman told Reuters.
