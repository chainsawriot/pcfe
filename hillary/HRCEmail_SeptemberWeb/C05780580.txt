Subject: Germany expels five Libyan diplomats (Reuters)

BERLIN, April 13 (Reuters) - Germany has expelled five Libyan diplomats for intimidating Libyan citizens
living in Germany, the Foreign Ministry said on Wednesday.

   The Libyan ambassador to Germany was also summoned to the Foreign Ministry. The five diplomats have to
leave Germany within seven days, the Foreign Ministry said.
