Yemen President Saleh Promises to Step Down in 2013, Xinhua Says
2011-01-23 17:20:48.86 GMT



By Dick Schumacher
  Jan. 23 (Bloomberg) -- Yemeni President All Abdullah Saleh said he won't seek re-election when his current term
expires in 2013, the Xinhua news service reported today, citing a speech by Saleh on state television.
  The announcement came after eight days of protests against Saleh, Xinhua said.
