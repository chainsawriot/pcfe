BENGHAZI (Reuters) - Libya's interim Prime Minister Mahmoud Jibril will be in Tripoli September 8, his
office said, on his first visit to the capital since Muammar Gaddafi was toppled after six months of civil war.

   Jibril, head of the National Transitional Council's executive office, will hold a news conference at 6 p.m.
(1600 GMT) in Tripoli, his office said in an emailed statement.
