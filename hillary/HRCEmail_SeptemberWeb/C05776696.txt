JERICHO (Reuters) - Russian President Medvedev on January 18 said Russia had recognized an independent
Palestinian state in 1988 and was not changing that position. Speaking at a news conference with Palestinian
President Abbas in Jericho, Medvedev said in Russia: "We made our decision then and we have not changed it
today."
