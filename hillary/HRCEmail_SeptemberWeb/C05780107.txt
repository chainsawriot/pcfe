                   The Embassy of the Argentine Republic presents its compliments to the
     Internal Revenue Service, International Division, and respectfully requests a clarification of
     Section 893 (26 CPR 1893-1) that regulates compensation of employees of foreign
     government or international organizations.

                    Specifically, this Embassy is interested in detexmining:

                  I- Whether the category of consular employees referred to in the Consular
      Agreement signed by .Argentina and the U.S. includes both Consulate and Embassy
r     employees.

                   II-Whether the Argentine or third countries citizens that are employed in this
     Mission and that are U.S. residents are exempted from the payment of Federal income tax
     whether or not they have filed the waiver provided by section 247 (b) of the Immigration
     and Nationality Act, 8 U.S.0 1257 (b) (1970).

                    The Embassy of the Argentine Republic avails itself of this opportunity to
     renew to the Internal Revenue Service, International Division, the assurances of its highest
     consideration.
