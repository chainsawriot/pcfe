Subject: Kuwait's ruler swears in new cabinet - TV (Reuters)


KUWAIT, May 8 (Reuters) - Kuwait's emir swore in a new cabinet on Sunday, privately owned al-Rai
television reported, after the previous government quit in March.

   There was no immediate word on the composition of the new government. The previous cabinet quit to .
avoid the questioning by parliament of three ministers, all members of the ruling al-Sabah family, amid calls for
political and economic reform.
