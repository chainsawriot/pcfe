Subject: Tunisian Islamist party leader returns home (AP)

TUNIS (AP) - The leader of a Tunisian Islamist party that was long outlawed by authorities has returned to his
homeland after two decades in exile.
  About 1,000 people crowded into the Tunis airport Sunday to welcome Rachid Ghanouchi, leader of the
Ennahdha, or Renaissance, party.
