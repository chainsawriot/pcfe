Subject: Italy says end of Gaddafi rule is Inevitable (Reuters)



ROME (Reuters) - The end of Libyan leader Muammar Gaddafi's rule was "inevitable", the foreign minister of
Italy, his closest European ally, said on Sunday. Foreign Minister Frattini also said a friendship and cooperation
treaty between Libya and Italy was "de facto suspended". "We have reached, I believe, a point of no return,"
Frattini commented. Asked whether Gaddafi should leave power, he said: "It is inevitable for this to happen.
