RAMALLAH (AP) - An official from the Fatah movement says it is nominating Prime Minister Salam Fayyad
to head a transitional Palestinian government as part of a unity deal with their rivals in the Islamic militant
group Hamas. Fatah members met June 11 to finalize a proposed list of candidates for Cabinet positions.
