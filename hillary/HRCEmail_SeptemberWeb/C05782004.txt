The Secretary's Call Sheet for
Transitional National Council Chairman Abdel Jalil


Purpose of call: To express American support and solidarity with Jalil and the TNC on the historic events unfolding in
Libya and to encourage him to use his leadership to support a peaceful, transparent, and inclusive transition to
democracy.




Background: TNC Chairman Abdel Jalil is the widely respected and mild-mannered leader of the Transitional National
Council, considered the "anti-Qadhafi." He announced the arrest of Qadhafi's son, Saif al-lslami, by rebel forces August
21 and is reportedly preparing now for an address to the Libyan people on the end of Qadhafi's reign. While the TNC
