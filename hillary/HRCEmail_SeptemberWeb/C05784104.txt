                                Pakistan's new Minister of Foreign Affairs


    On the advice of Prime Minister Syed YusufRaza Gilani, the President of Pakistan has decided
to elevate Ms. Hina Rabbani Khar as Federal Minister for Foreign Affairs.

   Ms. Hina Rabbani Khar took oath of office today at the Aiwan-e-Sadr as the 26th Minister of
Foreign Affairs of Pakistan. The oath was taken by Acting President Mr. Farooq H. Naik in the
absence of President Asif Ali Zardari who is on a visit to Afghanistan.

  Ms. Khar has the distinction of being the youngest and first woman Foreign Minister of
Pakistan.

   Minister of Foreign Affairs Hina Rabbani Khar is proceeding to Indonesia later this week to
lead the Pakistan delegation to the 18th ASEAN Regional Forum where she is expected to have
bilateral meetings with the Chinese Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Yang Jiechi, and US
Secretary of State Hillary Clinton. On return she will proceed to India for the Ministerial level
Dialogue in the Pakistan-India resumed dialogue process with her Indian counterpart Minister S
M Krishna.

    Members of the Cabinet and of the diplomatic corps in Islamabad as well as senior officials
attended the oath taking ceremony.
