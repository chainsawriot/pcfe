Subject: UN chief to join Quartet Mideast meeting Friday (Reuters)



UNITED NATIONS (Reuters) - U.N. Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon will take part in a meeting of the
"Quartet" of Middle East peace mediators at 2:30 p.m. Friday, the United Nations said.

   The Quartet, made up of the United Nations, the European Union, the United States and Russia, has been
working to agree on a statement that it hopes can set parameters for resumed direct talks between Israel and the
Palestinians.
