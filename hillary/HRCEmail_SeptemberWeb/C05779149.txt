CAIRO (Reuters) - The head of Egypt's ruling military council issued a decree on Tuesday ordering a
constitutional amendment committee to finish its work within 10 days, the state news agency
reported. Mohamed Hussein Tantawi also confirmed retired judge Tareq al-Bishry as head of the committee
tasked with proposing constitutional amendments.
