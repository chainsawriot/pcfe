Subject: Obama, Lebanese prime minister to meet January 12 (AP)

WASHINGTON (AP) - The White House says President Barack Obama will meet January 12 with Lebanese
Prime Minister Saad Hariri. Among the topics they are expected to discuss are political tensions in Lebanon
stemming from an international investigation into the 2005 assassination of the former prime minister, Rafik
Hariri. Obama and Hariri are also expected to discuss other regional issues.
