LIBYA UPDATE

SUDAN SAYS KADUGLI UNDER CONTROL WHILE CLASHES CONTINUE
(SBU) President al-Bashir said the Sudanese army was in control of Kadugli, a northern border state city,
media report. The Office of the Special Envoy for Sudan notes Sudanese government forces continue to clash
with the Sudanese People's Liberation Army (SPLA) in different locations across Southern Kordofan state. The
government has accused the SPLA of inciting violence in the region after it lost recent local elections. The
South Sudan government and SPLA stated they will not return to war with the north. (Ops/USSES e-mail,
Xinhua News)

ETHIOPIAN SUPREME COURT SENTENCES 14 TO JAIL FOR BOMB PLOT
(SBU) The individuals were sentenced on terrorism charges for plotting to bomb Addis Ababa during an AU
summit in January, according to press. The Ethiopia desk notes the court's ruling is consistent with government
policies to take a strong line against terrorism in the country. (Ops/Ethiopia desk telcon, AP)
