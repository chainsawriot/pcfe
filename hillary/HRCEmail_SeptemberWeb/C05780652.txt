BELGRADE (AP) - Serbian TV station B92 says authorities have arrested Goran Hadzic, the last remaining
fugitive sought by the UN war crimes court.
  Hadzic has been on the run for eight years. He is wanted for atrocities stemming from the 1991-1995 war in
Croatia.
