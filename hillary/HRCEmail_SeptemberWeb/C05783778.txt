The Secretary's Call Sheet for Afghanistan President Hamid Karzai

Purpose of Call: You are calling to express your condolences on the death of President Karzai's
half-brother and Kandahar Provincial Council Chairman, Ahmad Wali Karzai.




Background: Ahmad Wali Karzai was assassinated at his residence in Kandahar on July 12 at
11:15 a.m. local time. His alleged assailant was Dand District Afghan National Police
Checkpoint Commander, Sardar Mohammad. The Taliban claimed responsibility for the
act. The U.S. government is transporting President Karzai and much of his cabinet to Kandahar
this morning on two US aircrafts. Ahmand Wali Karzai was once a U.S. Legal Permanent
Resident, but is not an American citizen. Post is reaching out to Palace officials with
condolences and offers of assistance. The Embassy also issued a brief public statement.
