CAIRO (Reuters) - Reformist Mohamed ElBaradei said on a privately owned TV channel on Wednesday that
he intends to run for president in Egypt's 2011 presidential election.
   "When the door of presidential nominations opens, I intend to nominate myself," ElBaradei said on a live
talk show on ONTV channel.
