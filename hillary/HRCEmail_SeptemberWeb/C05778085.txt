TUNIS (Reuters) - Tunisia's Minister of Economic Reform, Elyes Jouini, has resigned, private radio broadcaster
Shems FM said on Tuesday. Jouini's departure follows three other high-profile resignations from the interim
government, including that of the prime minister, since a flare up of street protests.
