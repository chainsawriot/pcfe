Subject: Tunisian president fires interior minister (Reuters)

TUNIS (Reuters) - Tunisian President Ben Ali has replaced his interior minister and ordered the release of all
those detained in a wave of violent clashes. Prime Ministeraid Ghannouchi said the president had ordered the
creation of a special committee to investigate corruption and the actions of some officials. Ahmed Friaa, a
former academic and junior minister, was appointed as the new interior minister.
