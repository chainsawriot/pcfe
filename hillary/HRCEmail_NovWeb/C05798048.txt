WASHINGTON (AP) - Secretary of State Hillary Rodham Clinton will testify Jan. 23 before the House Foreign
Affairs Committee about the deadly Sept. 11 assault on the US mission in Libya.
  That's the word from Rep. Ed Royce, chairman of the panel. He said in a statement late Monday that Clinton
will answer questions about the raid that killed US Ambassador Chris Stevens and three other Americans in
Benghazi.
