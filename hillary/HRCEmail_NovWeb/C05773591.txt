POLITICO Breaking News

Commerce Secretary John Bryson informed the department's employees Thursday that he has given the
president a letter of resignation. "I have come to the conclusion that I need to step down to prevent distractions
from this critical mission," Bryson said. Bryson had been on a medical leave of absence since June 11,
following a seizure that resulted in his involvement in two car accidents.
