                                                  MEDIA NOTE

    The U.S. Department of State Once Again Ranks in Top 10 of 2012 Best Places to Work in the Federal
                                         Government Rankings

The Partnership for Public Service yesterday released the results of the 2012 Best Places to Work in the Federal
Government rankings, and the Department of State has once again ranked in the top 10, placing 3' overall
among the 19 large Federal agencies, and also ranked 3rd in effective leadership, strategic management, and
support for diversity: The Department leadership is very pleased the positive feedback from our employees
keeps us in the top 10.

The rankings measure federal employee satisfaction and commitment and are based on responses from more
than 700,000 federal workers. The Best Places to Work rankings include 292 federal agencies and
subcomponents — representing 33 percent of the 2.1 million person federal workforce.
