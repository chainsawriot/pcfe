UNITED NATIONS, May 27 (Reuters) - The U.N. Security Council will meet at 2:30 p.m. (1830 GMT) on
Sunday to discuss the massacre of at least 109 people, including dozens of children in Houla, Syria, a council
diplomat said.

   Facing mounting international outrage over the massacre, Syria accused rebels of carrying out the killings on
Friday.
