Subject: Palestinians protest outside President Mahmoud Abbas's headquarters (Al Jazeera)

(Al Jazeera) Around 30 Palestinians are protesting outside President Mahmoud Abbas's headquarters in
Ramallah ahead of US Secretary of State Hillary Clinton's visit in the West Bank, Al Jazeera's Rania Zabaneh
reports.

Following Abbas's meeting with Clinton, he will also meet with United Nations Secretary General Ban Ki-
Moon on Wednesday.

Meanwhile, Zabaneh is also reporting that journalists will hold a rally in Hebron to protest the attacks on the
press.

Three journalists who were killed by Israeli air strikes on their cars in Gaza on Tuesday.
