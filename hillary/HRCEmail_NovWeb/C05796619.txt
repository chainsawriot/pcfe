Subject: Annan to visit Moscow for Syria talks - ministry source (Reuters)

MOSCOW (Reuters) - U.N. special envoy Kofi Annan will visit Moscow on Monday for talks on the conflict in
Syria, RIA news agency quoted a Foreign Ministry source as saying on Friday.

   "The current state of affairs in Syria and prospects for a (peace) settlement will be discussed," the source
said, adding that Annan would meet Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov.
