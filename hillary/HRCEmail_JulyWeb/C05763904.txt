Tom shannon passed on the below points:
The Honduran Armed Forces released a statement late last evening. It makes the following points:
- The Armed Forces respect the Constitution and the laws and reaffirms its subordination to civil authority in conformity
with law and obedience.
- As an institution, the Armed Forces backs a solution to the crisis Honduras faces through a process of negotiation in
the framework of the San Jose Accords.
- Accordingly, we reiterate our unrestricted support for the results of the same, in accordance with the Constitution and
laws.
- The Armed Forces as an institution will continue to fulfill the missions given it to it by the Constitutions and the laws of
Honduras.
