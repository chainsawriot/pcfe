Subject: Update 2: Media Report Fatalities in Attack on Algerian Gas Facility (U)

(U) According to unconfirmed press reports, militants claim Algerian forces attacked the Algerian gas
facility. Up to 35 hostages and 15 hostage-takers were reportedly killed. Media did not specify the nationalities
of those allegedly killed.



From: OnsAlert@state.gov [mailto:OpsAlert(astate.gov]
Sent: Thursday, January 17, 2013 7:25 AM
Subject: Update 1: Foreigners Reportedly Escape Algerian Gas Facility (SBU)


(SBU) Embassy Algiers reports UK PM Cameron talked with Algerian PM Sellal, who confirmed there was a
planned intervention at the facility. Some of the capturers were killed or injured, some expatriates managed to
escape, and there is a possibility of fatalities among the hostages. The situation is still very fluid and there are
not yet confirmed numbers or nationalities.
