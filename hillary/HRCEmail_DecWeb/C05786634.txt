DAKAR (AP) - An official in Niger says Moammar Gadhafi's intelligence chief has surfaced in the West
African nation of Mali and one of the slain leader's sons is also on the way.
  The intelligence chief, Abdullah al-Senoussi, and Gadhafi's son Seif al-Islam, are wanted by the International
Criminal Court.
