From: OpsAlert

Subject: Update 3: Attacks in Norway

(SBU) A gunman dressed as a police officer injured at least five people at a government youth conference on
Utoya Island, about 30 miles from Oslo, according to press. Reports also indicate possible fatalities. Police
have arrested one suspect at the youth camp. Embassy Oslo notes the situation on the island is unclear, but
there are approximately 700 youths age 16 to 28 participating in the annual conference.

Subject: Update 2: Oslo Explosions Damage Prime Minister's Office

(SBU) Embassy Oslo reports the explosion was reportedly a vehicle-borne bomb between the buildings housing
the Prime Minister's office and the Oil and Energy Ministry. There are conflicting reports about whether there
was one or multiple bombs. Police are evacuating parts of downtown due to concerns about two possible
unexploded bombs in the area. Two people have been reported killed and at least seven hospitalized.

(SBU) Post has accounted for all American Chief of Mission personnel and has received no indication any
Embassy personnel were affected. There are no reports of American citizen casualties.
