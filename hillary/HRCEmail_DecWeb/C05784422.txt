Subject: Wikileaks founder wins legal round against extradition (SBU)

(SBU) The UK's High Court granted Julian Assange permission to appeal his extradition from the United
Kingdom to Sweden, media report. Assange's lawyer said he has 14 days to submit a written request to the
Supreme Court. Embassy London comments most observers expected the court to deny the appeal. There
likely will be a delay of several months while both sides file papers and wait for a decision.
