Subject: Update 3: American School in Tunis on Fire (SBU)

(SBU) Embassy    Tunis comments the school's director reports the school is on fire. However, all students and
staff were dismissed prior to the attack, and there is no one in the building at this time.

Embassy Tunis adds protesters have poured gasoline on part of the embassy compound wall. The embassy fire
alarm has sounded, but this appears to be due to smoke coming from outside the compound. Media report
armored vehicles and horse-mounted police are on site at the embassy.

(SBU) Ambassador Walles reports COM personnel at the compound are safe, sheltering in the safe haven.

compound walls. The Tunisian government advised it will send additional police forces. There appears to be
damage to embassy property.
