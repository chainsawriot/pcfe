(Irish Independent) - The seven-year-old daughter of Foreign Affairs Minister Micheal Martin has died. It is understood
Leana, the youngest daughter of Mr. Martin and his wife Mary, suffered from a heart condition. She died peacefully in
Great Ormond Street Hospital, London. Her parents and family were at her bedside. Mr. Martin and his wife said they
are heartbroken at the loss of their "darling Leana."
