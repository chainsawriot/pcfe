EVENTS ON THE GROUND
(U) There are initial reports Haitians are beginning to enter the Dominican Republic. The Dominican
authorities have stepped up immigration patrols fearing an influx of illegal immigrants. (hindustantimes.com)

U.S. COAST GUARD CONDUCTS INITIAL ASSESSMENT OF HARBOR
(SBU) Pier structures in the harbor appear to have collapsed with numerous oil sheens on the water. The only
known container crane and all other port cranes appear to have been damaged with some being submerged. The
port is not crowded, with approximately 15 small commercial and fishing vessels underway. (USCG SitRep)
