Senate Majority Leader Harry Reid's (D-Nev.) wife and daughter were in a car accident Thursday and taken to
a Washington-area hospital.

"Today Sen. Reid's wife, Landra, and daughter, Lana, were involved in an accident in which their vehicle was
rear-ended. They are both being treated at a Washington-area hospital," Reid's office said in a statement
released to CNN.

Additional details were not yet known.
